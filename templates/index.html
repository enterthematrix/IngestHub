StreamSets Self-Service Ingestion

Democratization Your Data

How it works?

  • The app allows users to take advantage of pre-built StreamSets Job templates to ingest data to/from a variety of data sources.
  • No in-depth StreamSets knowledge required.
  • Users will be able to pick from a range of origin and destinations they want to work with.
  • Based on the origin/destination selection, a pre-defined template will be selected.
  • Users will be able to change the runtime configurations for the origin/destination [except static parameters].
  • On submit, the app will launch a new StreamSets job using the specified parameters.
  • The completed job details can be viewed on the jobs page.

Benefits of Self-Service Data Ingestion

  • Self-service ingestion allows enterprises to move away from a fragmented approach to data capturing, enrichment, and processing.
  • Empowers everyone in an organization to make data-driven decisions by making a broad range of data sources available for analytics purposes.
  • Empowers non-technical users to replicate data for their analytics needs.
  • Empowers users to focus on high-value analytics vs creating/maintaining ETL jobs.
  • Self-Service data ingestion is inherently scalable and performant.
  • Democratize new data sources faster and at-scale.
